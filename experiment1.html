IMPORTANCE OF RAILWAYS:

Indian Railway provides the most important mode of public transport in India. This is the most commonly used and cost effective long distance transport system of the country. Indian Railway is operating by Ministry of Railways. Indian Railways is touching life of almost every people across India covering 28 states and 3 Union Territories with its 40,050 miles or 64,460 kilometers.
